SERVICES

Accommodation

Comfortable and well-appointed rooms with various options to suit different preferences and budgets. Accommodation in a hotel typically refers to a place where guests can stay overnight or for an extended period of time while they are away from home. Hotels usually offer a variety of room types and amenities to meet the needs and preferences of different guests.

Food Service/ Food Runner

On-site restaurants offering delicious and diverse cuisine, including local specialties and international dishes. Food Service or a Food Runner in a hotel typically refers to an individual responsible for delivering food orders to guests in various areas of the hotel, such as restaurants, room service, or banquets.

Business and Conference

Fully-equipped meeting rooms and event spaces for corporate gatherings, conferences, and other social events. Business and conference services in a hotel refer to the range of amenities and facilities provided to meet the needs of business travelers and corporate groups. These services may include meeting rooms, conference rooms, audiovisual equipment, high-speed internet access, business centers, printing and copying facilities, and catering services.

Concierge & Guest Services

24/7 front desk and concierge services that cater to guests' needs, such as arranging transportation, booking tours, and providing local recommendations. Concierge and guest services in a hotel refer to the personalized assistance and support provided to guests by the hotel staff. This may include a wide range of services, such as making restaurant reservations, arranging transportation, providing directions and recommendations for local attractions, or assisting with travel arrangements.

Housekeeping & Maintenance

Daily housekeeping services to ensure rooms are clean and tidy, as well as maintenance services to address any issues that may arise. Housekeeping and maintenance services in a hotel refer to the teams responsible for cleaning, maintaining and repairing the hotel's rooms, facilities, and common areas. These services are essential for ensuring that the hotel is clean, functional, and safe for guests.
